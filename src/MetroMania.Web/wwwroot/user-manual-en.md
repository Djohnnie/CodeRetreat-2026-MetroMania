# Metro Mania

## Coding Challenge

**Metro Mania** is a coding challenge where you build a bot that plays a metro network management game.

### Objective

Connect multiple metro stations with metro lines and keep your metro network alive by ensuring all passengers reach their destination.

### How It Works

- **Stations** appear on the map, each represented by a specific type (shape).
- **Passengers** spawn at stations and need to travel to a station matching their desired type.
- Your bot must **draw metro lines** connecting stations to form an efficient network.
- Passengers will board trains and travel along your lines to reach a station that matches their destination type.

### The Challenge

Keep your network running smoothly! If too many passengers are left waiting at a station without a route to their destination, the game ends. Strategically plan your metro lines to handle growing demand and keep all passengers moving.
